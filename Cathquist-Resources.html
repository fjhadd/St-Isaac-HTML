Virtual Classes Roles

Lead Catechist (LC)

The lead catechist is responsible for preparing and teaching the class.

Monitor Catechist (MC)

Be available for students and assist with any questions they might have during the class.

Review Catechist (RC)

Review student weekly assignments and provide feedback.

Assistant Catechist (AC)

Follow up with students that missed class or did not submit the assignments.

Catechist Coordinator (CC)

Catechist Coordinator responsible to assist catechist with questions, issues, etc. Update supporting sheets and make sure everyone knows their roles for the week.
